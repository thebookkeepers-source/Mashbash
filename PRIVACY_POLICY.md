# Mashbash Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 7 June 2026

Mashbash is a food ordering and delivery application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how users can contact us.

## Information We Collect

Mashbash may collect the following information when users create an account, place an order, or use the app:

- Name
- Email address
- Mobile number
- Delivery address
- Order details
- Payment method selected by the user
- App/device notification token for push notifications
- Basic app usage and order status information

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Process food orders
- Deliver orders to customers
- Show order status and order history
- Notify customers, restaurant staff, and riders about order updates
- Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
- Prevent misuse, fraud, and unauthorized access

## Push Notifications

Mashbash may send push notifications for order updates, new orders, rider assignment, delivery updates, and important service messages. Users may disable notifications from their device settings.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. User information is shared only as needed to operate the food ordering service, such as showing delivery details to restaurant staff or assigned riders.

## Data Storage and Security

Mashbash uses secure backend services to store app data. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

## User Control

Users may contact us to request correction or deletion of their personal information, subject to legal, security, and operational requirements.

## Children’s Privacy

Mashbash is intended for general food ordering use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

## Third-Party Services

Mashbash may use third-party services for backend infrastructure, authentication, storage, analytics, and push notifications. These services may process information according to their own privacy and security practices.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
